---
title: Why are you interested in this role?
category: non-technical
subcategory: career
difficulty: easy
hint: Tie company, role, and your goals together.
tags: []
---

Give a specific, authentic answer covering:

1. **The work** — what excites you about the problems or product.
2. **The team/company** — culture, mission, or tech that aligns with you.
3. **Your growth** — how the role fits your next career step.

Avoid generic praise. Show you've researched the company and connect your existing skills to their needs.
