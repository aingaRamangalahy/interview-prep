---
title: Tell me about yourself.
category: non-technical
subcategory: behavioral
difficulty: easy
hint: Present → past → future, under 2 minutes.
tags: []
---

Structure a concise narrative:

1. **Present** — current role and core strengths.
2. **Past** — relevant experience that led here.
3. **Future** — why this role/company fits your goals.

Keep it under two minutes, focus on outcomes and skills relevant to the job, and end by connecting your background to the position.
